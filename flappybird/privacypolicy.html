Privacy Policy

Fly Bird Fly does not receive or use any personal information from your device.

There are no ads in this app or any in-app purchases. We do not ask for or use any user information.

The app does not have any interaction between multiple users, and does not store any information about the user (personal or otherwise) on any cloud/server.

Users should note that downloading an app from the Play Store automatically stores basic details about your device and general location; however this information is not available to the developer in a "per-person view", instead it is used to generate general statistics about the various devices and android versions being used to download the app from different regions around the world.
